The first automatic home plant-watering system.

 

Started in 2018 by a 13-year old girl who had an idea. She knew that her plants would always die because everyone forgot to water them.

She thought she should make a plant watering system that can be easily accessed by your phone because usually everyone has their phone on them.

She made the iWater one day by just brainstorming ideas. Now this product is being sold world-wide to all different consumers.
